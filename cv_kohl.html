Curriculum Vitae

Stefan Kohl

July 10, 1973 Born in Sindelfingen / Germany.
September 1980 - August 1984 Elementary school in Weil der Stadt / Germany.
September 1984 - May 1993 Johannes-Kepler-Gymnasium in Weil der Stadt.
March 25, 1993 2nd price in the Landeswettbewerb Jugend forscht in Baden-Württemberg on the subject mathematics / computer science for the project Programmierung des ostasiatischen Brettspiels Go. (Download)
May 18, 1993 Abitur at the Johannes-Kepler-Gymnasium in Weil der Stadt.
October 1993 - February 2000 Studied mathematics with minor subject computer science at the University of Stuttgart.
March 8, 1996 Vordiplom in mathematics.
February 28, 2000 Diplom in mathematics.
Thesis: Über die Anzahl der Bahnen auf endlichen Gruppen unter der Operation der Automorphismengruppe - Suzuki-Gruppen vs. lineare Gruppen (Counting the Orbits on Finite Groups under the Action of the Automorphism Group - Suzuki Groups vs. Linear Groups).
Advisor: Prof. Dr. Markus Stroppel.
Download: DVI PDF.
March 2000 - October 2005 Doktorand in mathematics at the University of Stuttgart. Advisor: Prof. Dr. Wolfgang Kimmerle.
August 18 - September 8, 2001 Invited visit at the University of St Andrews / Scotland.
Purpose: Introduction to GAP development. Since then, one of the GAP developers.
Since July 2003 Member of the GAP Support Group.
October 6, 2005 Dr. rer. nat. in mathematics.
Thesis: Restklassenweise affine Gruppen (Residue-Class-Wise Affine Groups).
Download of the slides I used in my talk in the public part of the examination: PDF PS.
Since then Further research; found and investigated a simple group which arises in a natural way from the arithmetical structure of the ring of integers. Cf. the article A Simple Group Generated by Involutions Interchanging Residue Classes of the Integers, which appeared in 2009 in Mathematische Zeitschrift.
August 1 - October 31, 2007 Developed teaching material for the computer algebra system GAP for the University of St Andrews.
October 2008 - September 2010 Profesor i Ftuar (Visiting Professor), Universiteti Ismail Qemali Vlorë / Albania.
June 26, 2010 Organized a session on computational group theory on the conference ACA 2010, Applications of Computer Algebra, with talks by Max Horn (Germany), Vasyl Alex Ustimenko (Poland), Ekaterina Khrebtova (Russia, Dubai), Thomas Bier (Oman), Sandeep Rajkumar Murthy (Great Britain) and Urszula Romanczuk (Poland). (Jointly with Dmitry Malinin.)
September 6, 2010 Obtained albanian title Profesor i Asociuar, registered by the Ministry of Education and Sciences of the Republic of Albania.
March 2011 - July 2012 Profesor i Ftuar (Visiting Professor), Universiteti Ismail Qemali Vlorë / Albania.
