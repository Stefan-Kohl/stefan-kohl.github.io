MAT 452: Introduction to Algebra II (Spring 2012)

Stefan Kohl

Course Description

The course sequence MAT 451 / 452 provides an introduction to groups, rings and fields.

Literature (among many other good books on the topic):

  • David S. Dummit, Richard M. Foote: Abstract Algebra. John Wiley and Sons, 2004.
  • Serge Lang: Algebra. Springer GTM 211, 2002.
  • Thomas W. Judson: Abstract Algebra -- Theory and Applications.
    http://abstract.ups.edu/download.html

Software: GAP (Groups, Algorithms and Programming) -- a system for computational discrete algebra.
