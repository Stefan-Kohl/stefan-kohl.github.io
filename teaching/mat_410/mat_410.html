MAT 410: Introduction to Topology (Fall 2009)

Stefan Kohl

Course Description

The course is a standard introduction to topology. It follows the script by David R. Wilkins (Trinity College Dublin) which is available on Wilkins' homepage here.

Recommended literature: Klaus Jaenich: Topology, Springer, 1984.
