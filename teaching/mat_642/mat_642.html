MAT 642: Computational Algebra II (Spring 2010)

Course Description

This course is the continuation of MAT 641: Computational Algebra I from Fall 2009. It follows the book Ideals, Varieties, and Algorithms: An Introduction to Computational Algebraic Geometry and Commutative Algebra by David Cox, John Little and Donal O'Shea.
