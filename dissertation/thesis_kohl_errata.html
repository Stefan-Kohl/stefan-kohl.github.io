Errata for Thesis Restklassenweise affine Gruppen

This page lists the currently known errata for the thesis Restklassenweise affine Gruppen. The list is sorted by page numbers.

  • In the english summary on page vii in line 10, 11 and 16, it must read 'transitively' instead of 'transitive'.
  • The assertion given in the english summary on page viii in line 13-15 resp. in the german summary on page xiii in line 17-19 is slightly stronger than what is actually shown in Theorem 3.11 and Remark 3.12.
  • In Beispiele 1.1.3, Teil (1) on page 2 it must read '{(2n-1)/3,2n}' instead of '{(3n-1)/2,2n}'.
  • On page 75 in the second-last line the phrase 'und es sei ...' is not wrong, but superfluous.
  • On page 79 in line 3 of Section B.1, it is wrongly claimed that in Beispiele 1.8.5, Teil (4) it would be conjectured that α and β generate a free group of rank 2. (The conjecture itself may be true or not -- I don't know.)
  • On page 97, the word 'packages' in the URL of my GAP package RCWA must be capitalized.
    Remark: The correct URL http://www.gap-system.org/Packages/rcwa.html is given in the english as well as in the german summary on page viii resp. xiii.

For convenience, the errors listed above are corrected in the files provided for download on this site. Since the thesis is originally written in German, there is also a german version of this page.
