GAP Crossword Puzzle

Stefan Kohl

In the GAP session log below, any term - identifier or keyword - to be filled in into the crossword puzzle has been replaced by number and direction, enclosed in angular brackets. For example, <44R> stands for number 44, to the right and <32D> stands for number 32, down. Terms are case-insensitive, and end at bold lines - so for example <7D> has 8 characters. For printing, you can download the graphics in full resolution (1440 x 1200) and the text as a PDF file.
